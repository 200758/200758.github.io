1. 1995-1996 Chicago Bulls

They were the best team in all of NBA History. The team average 105.2 points per game and allowed 92.2 points per contest, had the top offensive rating in the league which is 115.2. They ended up with a composite elo rating of 1815. Their final regular-season record was 72-10.

2. 1996-1997 Chicago Bulls

Chicago Bulls was also a really good team during 1996-1997. They had the second best record. Their composite elo was 1802 which was almost the same as their composite elo during 1995-1996. Their final regular-season record was 69-13.
