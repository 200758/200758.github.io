SKILLS TO GET PAST OPPONENTS IN A GAME

Here are a few ways to get past an opponent in basketball

1. Once you see an opponent in front of you, you can do a few crossovers to confuse him or her, then go to you your right and suddenly turn the other way to lose him or her. You can turn any way you want, but this way is easier to get past them.

2. If the first method does not work, then use this method. You can dribble up to him and ask one of your teammates to screen for you. You can get past them easier since they need time to chase after you.
