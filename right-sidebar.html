1. Michael Jordan

He is the greatest NBA player of all time. His insane 1988 season went like this: 35 points on 53% shooting, 5.5 rebounds, 5.9 assists, 3.2 steals and 1.6 blocks.

2. Bill Russell

He is the second best player in NBA history and is also the best defender in NBA history.His career playoff stats were 16.2 PPG, 24.9 RPG, 4.7 APG.

3. Magic Johnson

He is 6 foot and 9 inches tall and he uses his his large frame to shred defenses with precision.
