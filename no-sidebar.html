BEFORE VS AFTER PLAYING BASKETBALL

Basketball is a sport that makes you grow taller and stronger and is a healthy sport

Growth

Basketball makes you taller and stronger. When you jump to shoot a basketball or when you try to defend a basketball from getting into the basket, it makes you become taller. Jumping is a way you can grow taller. Most Basketball Players are tall because of all the training they did and number of times they dunked, shot and blocked baskets.

Playing basketball can also make you stronger and make you have more muscles. Your strength increases when your hands are dribbling the ball and doing crossovers and also your legs are getting stronger when you run like other sports.
